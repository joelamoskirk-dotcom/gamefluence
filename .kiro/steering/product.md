# Product Overview

Gamefluence is a gaming creator marketing platform that connects APAC agencies and brands with gaming content creators and influencers for authentic audience engagement.

## Core Features

- **Agency Dashboard**: Multi-client campaign management with white-label options
- **Creator Discovery**: Browse and select gaming influencers with APAC market expertise
- **Brand Campaign Builder**: Cross-brand campaign creation with localized targeting
- **Performance Analytics**: Real-time ROI tracking and attribution across markets
- **Payment & Billing**: Integrated payment system with multi-currency support

## Target Users

- **APAC Marketing Agencies**: Primary users managing multiple brand clients
- **Regional Brands**: Companies wanting to reach gaming audiences across APAC markets
- **Gaming Content Creators**: Influencers monetizing their APAC audience reach

## Key Value Propositions

- **Agency-First Platform**: Built for agencies managing multiple brand campaigns
- **APAC Market Expertise**: Deep understanding of Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia markets
- **Authentic Creator Network**: Verified gaming influencers with local market knowledge
- **Cross-Market Attribution**: AppsFlyer-style tracking across APAC regions
- **White-Label Solutions**: Agencies can brand the platform for their clients

## User Journey

1. **Agency Onboarding**: Set up multi-client dashboard with brand portfolios
2. **Market Selection**: Choose target APAC markets (Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia)
3. **Creator Matching**: AI-powered matching with local gaming influencers
4. **Campaign Execution**: Launch coordinated campaigns across multiple markets
5. **Performance Reporting**: Real-time analytics and client reporting dashboards

## Brand Guidelines

- **Professional Agency Focus**: Clean, data-driven interface design
- **APAC Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect for local markets and cultural nuances
- **Performance-Driven Messaging**: Emphasize ROI, attribution, and measurable results
- **Multi-Market Capability**: Highlight cross-border campaign management
- **Enterprise-Grade Reliability**: Professional tone with gaming industry expertise
